A pottery Studio dedicated to creativy and comunity

Sarah Glass ceramics is a woman-owned pottery studio located in fort Lauderdale. Our mission is to provide everyone of all skill levels a strong foundation and supportive environment to experience the relaxing art of pottery making. We are happy to provide studio space and equipment to let you channel your inner artist
